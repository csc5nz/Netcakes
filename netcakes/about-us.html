What is Netcakes?

Welcome to Netcakes, a new way to order cakes. No more need to go to the bakery and try to explain to the baker what kind of cake your want. Now, with Netcakes you can design your cake online and we will prepare it and deliver it directtly to your door.

We have a intuitive and easy to use tool that lets you choose every aspect of your cake such as type of layes, fillings, icing, and greetings. Once you're happy with your design, you can order with your most conveniet type of payment. Our master bakers will craft your cake base on your specifications. Finally, we will hand deliver your cake with care directly to your front door. Try Netcakes today.
